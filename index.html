Case Study 03 // Omnichannel Agility

+5% Total Sales Growth via Agile Real-Time Routing

SITUATION

Widespread global lockdowns during the pandemic resulted in sudden, catastrophic bricks-and-mortar store closures and near-zero retail footprint traffic, threatening multi-million dollar seasonal inventory write-offs.

TASK

Uncover alternative hyper-local revenue funnels, bridge the stock-availability gap, and safely bring product discovery directly to consumers when they were physically unable to visit brick-and-mortar storefronts.

Recovered massive revenue leakage lines that were otherwise written off due to static store disruption, scaling localized direct-to-home sales vectors to lift overall enterprise sales by 5%.
