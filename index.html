Growth Story 01 // Product Marketing & Market Entry

Launching Oliver Safety Footwear into the UK Market

Impact Indicator

US$1.5M First-Year Distributor Order

SITUATION

Oliver was a major footwear brand in Australia, but had limited traction in Europe because the product styling was considered better suited to Australian customers than mainland European buyers.

INSIGHT

Market study and sales analysis showed that UK customers preferred safety footwear styles closer to Australia than mainland Europe, creating a focused UK opportunity.

ACTION

Built the UK launch proposition around five priority SKUs, aligned product positioning with the commercial team and supported distributor engagement, in-store promotions and merchandising across 70+ UK retail stores.

RESULT

Secured a first annual distributor order worth US$1.5M, displaced a competing brand within the distributor portfolio and increased UK market share.
